Clean Water Initiative

We believe that sustainable work is locally-led. Along with implementing community-owned water projects, our local partners help facilitate comprehensive water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) programming to protect everyone’s long-term health.

771 million people lack basic access to clean and safe drinking water. We’re on a mission to change that.

Construction Projects

Multi-trade construction is a construction process that involves multiple skilled trades working together to complete a project. This approach streamlines the construction process by combining different trades, such as plumbing, electrical, and carpentry, into a single team.

The team works collaboratively, with each trade completing their specialized tasks in a coordinated manner.

International Shipping

We transport emergency supplies, equipment, and personnel to disaster-stricken areas across the globe. We work with logistics providers to coordinate and manage the transportation of goods, from packaging to delivery.

With your support, we can provide critical aid and support to those affected by disasters around the world.
